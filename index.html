How to read this map

  • Navy circle — a vulnerable road user (pedestrian, cyclist, or scooter rider) was struck.
  • Rust diamond — a vehicle-only crash or other incident on a corridor of interest. No one on foot or bike was hit.
  • A larger dot marks a fatality; a mid-size dot marks a child victim.

What it does and doesn't claim

  • Dots sit at the intersection, not the exact point of impact — they're approximate.
  • VRU strikes are tracked citywide. Vehicle-only crashes are tracked only on Braddock Road and Mount Vernon Avenue, and systematically only since May 2026.
  • Click any dot to open its card and hear the original dispatch audio. See how this is gathered and the underlying dataset.
